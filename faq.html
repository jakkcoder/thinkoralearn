Frequently Asked Questions

Are the courses live or recorded?

We use a blend of live interactive sessions and structured recorded content for flexibility.

Do you provide placement support?

Yes — resume reviews, mock interviews, portfolio feedback, and referrals.

Can beginners join?

Absolutely. We start from scratch and build up to advanced topics with projects.

What are the prerequisites?

Basic computer literacy is enough. We cover programming and tools from the ground up.

Do you offer 1:1 mentorship?

Yes, optional 1:1 sessions are available for doubt-solving and career guidance.

How long does it take to complete a course?

Data Analyst Champion takes 10-12 weeks, and Data Science takes 12-16 weeks. You can learn at your own pace with flexible scheduling.

What is the course fee structure?

We offer flexible payment options including installments. Contact us for detailed pricing and available scholarships or early-bird discounts.

Do I get a certificate upon completion?

Yes, you receive a course completion certificate along with project portfolio that showcases your practical skills to potential employers.

What kind of projects will I work on?

Real-world projects including data analysis dashboards, machine learning models, business case studies, and capstone projects that you can showcase in your portfolio.

Is there any coding experience required?

No prior coding experience needed. We start with programming fundamentals and gradually progress to advanced topics with plenty of practice exercises.

What software and tools will I need?

We'll guide you through installing free tools like Python, Jupyter Notebook, and various libraries. A standard laptop/computer with internet connection is sufficient.

How much time should I dedicate per week?

We recommend 8-12 hours per week for optimal progress, but you can adjust based on your schedule. The course is designed to be flexible for working professionals.

What kind of career support do you provide?

Comprehensive career support including resume building, LinkedIn profile optimization, mock interviews, portfolio reviews, and job referrals to our partner companies.

Can I access course materials after completion?

Yes, you get lifetime access to all course materials, recorded sessions, and future updates. You can revisit content anytime for reference.

What if I miss a live session?

All live sessions are recorded and made available within 24 hours. You can catch up at your convenience and still participate in Q&A through our discussion forum.

Do you provide job guarantees?

While we don't guarantee specific job placements, we provide extensive career support and have a strong track record of helping students land roles in data analytics and data science.

What's the difference between Data Analyst and Data Science courses?

Data Analyst focuses on data analysis, visualization, and business insights. Data Science covers advanced topics like machine learning, model deployment, and data engineering in addition to analysis.

Is the course suitable for working professionals?

Absolutely! Our courses are designed with working professionals in mind. Weekend live sessions, flexible scheduling, and self-paced learning make it easy to balance with your job.

What kind of support is available during the course?

24/7 email support, live doubt resolution sessions, peer discussion forums, 1:1 mentorship sessions, and dedicated support from subject matter experts.

Can I switch between courses if needed?

Yes, if you find that another course better fits your career goals, we offer course switching options. Contact our counseling team to discuss the best path for you.

What industries can I work in after completing the course?

Data skills are valuable across industries: finance, healthcare, e-commerce, consulting, marketing, technology, retail, and many more. Our alumni work in diverse sectors.

Do you offer group discounts or corporate training?

Yes, we offer special pricing for groups and customized corporate training programs. Contact us to discuss enterprise solutions for your organization.
